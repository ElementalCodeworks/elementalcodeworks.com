About

Creating digital experiences since 2004, Elemental CodeWorks Inc. (ECI) is a Canadian Controlled Private Corporation located in Fredericton, New Brunswick, Canada. ECI is owned and operated by Jacob Sanford, who has over 20 years of expertise in the field. We specialize in data-driven web experiences, LLM-based interfaces, and projects focused on revitalizing cultures, particularly Indigenous languages.

As the owner, I enjoy contributing to interesting and impactful projects. ECI has limited availability, which offers me the luxury of choosing my clients. I gravitate towards interesting projects, especially if its stakeholders share my interests and goals. Together we have crafted digital experiences that are accessible, usable, and delightful.
